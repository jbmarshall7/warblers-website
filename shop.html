Shipping & pickup

How you get your mead

Two ways, and a few rules that keep everything honest and legal:

  • We ship within Connecticut. Outside CT? Join the mailing list — we’ll say the moment that changes.
  • Free local pickup in Newtown, CT. Choose pickup at checkout and we’ll email you to arrange a time.
  • 21+ only. You confirm your age to enter the shop and again at checkout — then adult signature on delivery, or photo ID at pickup.
  • No PO boxes — carriers require a street address for alcohol.
