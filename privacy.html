The fine print

Privacy policy

The short version: we collect as little as possible, we don’t sell anything about you, and most of what the site remembers stays on your own device.

Last updated: July 18, 2026 · Warblers Meadery LLC, Newtown, Connecticut · hello@warblersmeadery.com

What we collect, and why

Contact form. When you write to us we store your name, email, topic, and message so we can reply. That’s the only thing we use them for.

The guestbook mural. If you place a patch, we store your drawing and/or signature, the name you optionally add, and a random visitor identifier (so the wall can limit patches per visitor). Approved patches are displayed publicly on this site. Don’t include personal details you wouldn’t want on a public wall.

Ordering. Checkout collects your name, email, and — for shipping — your address and state, used to fulfil the order and to meet alcohol-shipping rules (21+, adult signature, Connecticut only).

On your device only. Your cart, your age-gate confirmation, and your own in-review guestbook patches live in your browser’s local storage. They never leave your device and you can clear them any time in your browser settings.

What we don’t do

  • No selling or renting your information — ever.
  • No advertising trackers and no analytics cookies.
  • No marketing email unless you join the mailing list yourself, and every email will have an unsubscribe link.

Who helps us run the site

Warblersmeadery.com is hosted by Netlify and our data is stored with Supabase; like any web host they process technical logs (such as IP addresses) to serve and secure the site. Our typefaces load from Google Fonts, which receives the standard technical request data. None of them are permitted to use your information for their own marketing.

Age

This site sells alcohol and is intended for adults 21 and over. We don’t knowingly collect information from anyone under 21.

Your choices

Want a guestbook patch taken down, a message deleted, or a copy of what we hold about you? Email hello@warblersmeadery.com and we’ll sort it out.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version appears here with a new date at the top.
