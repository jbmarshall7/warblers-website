Guestbook

Draw or sign your own patch of a shared wall — a mural the whole flock builds together. Every patch gets a look from us before it goes up.

Demonstration mural. Patches are stored in this browser only (localStorage) — before launch, connect a small backend so every visitor shares one wall, and moderate there. Admins: open guestbook.html?admin=1 on this device to review.

Drag anywhere on the wall to claim a patch (up to 4 × 3 tiles) — overlapping is fine; patches layer like posters. Or let us pick a spot for you.

How it works

Claim a patch, leave a tune

Drag anywhere on the wall to fence off your patch — or tap “Pick a free spot for me” — then draw and/or sign your name inside it. What’s already on the wall shows through underneath, so you can draw right up against your neighbours.

Patches can overlap — they layer like posters on a wall, newest on top, and the flock curates which sit forward. Add as many as you like (within reason); every patch is held for a quick review before it appears, and only you can see yours while it waits.

Spot something off-key? Tap the little flag on a patch to report it, and we’ll take a look.

This is a family wall — no health claims, and keep it 21+-appropriate in spirit.
