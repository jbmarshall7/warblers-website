Terms of use

The house rules for using warblersmeadery.com — plain-language, honey included.

21 and over

Mead is alcohol. By using the shop you confirm you are at least 21 years old. Deliveries require an adult signature (21+); pickups require photo ID. We may refuse or cancel any order where age can’t be verified. Please enjoy responsibly — and never drink and drive.

Orders, shipping & pickup

We currently sell and ship only within Connecticut, with free local pickup in Newtown by arrangement. We can’t ship to PO boxes. Orders may be limited or cancelled where the law requires, and prices and availability can change — small batches really do run out.
